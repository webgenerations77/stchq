Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Our Commitment

Spinach the Cow is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We believe that everyone deserves equal access to information and functionality on our website, regardless of disability or impairment. This aligns with our core values of helping others and building an inclusive community.

Standards We Follow

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including:

  • Visual impairments (blindness, low vision, color blindness)
  • Hearing impairments
  • Motor impairments
  • Cognitive and learning disabilities

What We've Done

To make our website as accessible as possible, we have implemented the following measures:

  • Semantic HTML: We use proper heading structure and semantic elements for screen reader compatibility
  • Alt text: All meaningful images include descriptive alternative text
  • Color contrast: We maintain sufficient color contrast ratios for text readability
  • Keyboard navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard
  • Responsive design: Our website adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels
  • ARIA labels: Interactive elements include appropriate ARIA attributes for assistive technologies
  • Focus indicators: Visible focus styles help keyboard users navigate the site

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas of the site may have limitations:

  • Some third-party content or embedded services may not fully meet accessibility standards
  • Older PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Some product images from third-party platforms may lack detailed alt text

We are actively working to address these limitations.

Feedback & Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: spinachthecow@gmail.com
  • Location: Ocean City, MD

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days and to resolve issues as quickly as possible.

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review and update our website to improve accessibility and welcome any input from our community. Being curious and always learning means we're committed to getting better at this every day.
